UNIVERSAL HIGHLIGHT CATEGORIES:

1. VICTORY/COMPLETION (Score 9-10):
   MODERN GAMES:
   - Text: "VICTORY", "YOU WIN", "WINNER", "MATCH WON"
   - Victory screens, trophies, podiums, rank displays
   - Scoreboards showing 1st place

   RETRO/PLATFORMER GAMES:
   - Level completion screens (e.g., "LEVEL CLEAR", "COURSE CLEAR")
   - Boss defeat animations or screens
   - 100% completion stats (kills, items, secrets all at 100%)
   - Flag at top of pole (Mario-style)
   - Victory fanfare visuals

   Example: "HANGAR FINISHED - KILLS 100%" or flag pole at maximum height

2. COMBAT HIGHLIGHTS:
   MODERN SHOOTERS (Score 7-9):
   - Kill feed: "Double Kill", "Triple Kill", "Headshot", "YOU ELIMINATED X"
   - Damage numbers, hit markers
   - Score popup after elimination

   PLATFORMER/RETRO (Score 7-9):
   - Enemy clearly being defeated (stomped, hit, exploding)
   - Multiple enemies defeated in quick succession (visual chain)
   - Boss taking visible damage (health bar dropping)
   - Enemy death animations clearly visible

   Example: Enemy flashing/disappearing after being stomped

3. POWER-UPS/ACHIEVEMENTS (Score 7-9):
   MODERN:
   - Achievement popup, "Level Up", trophy notifications
   - Legendary item drops with special effects

   RETRO/PLATFORMER:
   - Mushroom/star/flower appearing
   - Character transformation (small to big, color change)
   - Invincibility star effects (flashing colors)
   - Special items appearing (1-UP, rare collectibles)
   - Character glowing or sparkling after power-up

   Example: Mario growing from small to big, or star making character flash colors

4. EPIC ACTION MOMENTS (Score 7-9):
   MODERN:
   - Massive explosions, ultimate abilities
   - Critical health (<20% HP) during combat
   - Clutch plays (red screen, low HP bar)

   PLATFORMER/RETRO:
   - Perfect jump over multiple hazards
   - Character mid-air with multiple enemies below
   - Near-miss with enemy/hazard (very close)
   - Secret area discovery (hidden blocks, bonus rooms)
   - Precision platforming (narrow ledges, moving platforms)

   Example: Mid-air jump over 3 enemies in a row

5. FAILS/FUNNY MOMENTS (Score 6-8):
   - Character death in unusual way
   - Falling into pit/lava/hazard
   - Ragdoll physics, glitches
   - Unexpected enemy behavior

   Example: Character missing jump and falling

VISUAL INDICATORS TO LOOK FOR:

UI-BASED (Modern games):
- Kill feed text in corners
- Score popups, damage numbers
- Health bars, ammo counts
- Achievement notifications

ACTION-BASED (All games, especially retro):
- Enemy defeat animations (flashing, exploding, disappearing)
- Character transformations or power-up effects
- Dramatic visual effects (stars, sparkles, flashes)
- Score increasing dramatically
- Character in dangerous position (surrounded, mid-air)
- Multiple enemies on screen in combat
- Special visual effects (invincibility, fire, ice)

WHAT IS NOT A HIGHLIGHT (Score 0-3):
- Walking/running in empty area
- Normal movement without enemies
- Menu/inventory screens
- Loading screens
- Character standing still
- Generic level start screens
- Collecting single coin/ring
- Normal jumping without context

CRITICAL ANALYSIS STEPS:
1. Identify game type: Modern shooter? Retro platformer? Action game?
2. Look for TEXT first (kill feeds, achievements, victory)
3. Look for VISUAL ACTION (enemies being defeated, power-ups, transformations)
4. Check CHARACTER STATE (health, power-up status, position)
5. Be skeptical - if uncertain, score it low

SCORING RULES:
- 10/10: Clear victory screen, 100% completion, major achievement
- 8-9/10: Enemy defeat with clear confirmation (text OR visual), power-up transformation, boss defeat
- 5-7/10: Action happening but no clear highlight (jumping near enemy, combat without confirmation)
- 1-4/10: Normal movement, exploring, no action
- 0/10: Menu, loading, static scene
